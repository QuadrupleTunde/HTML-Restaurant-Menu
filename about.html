About Us: H-Thai-ML is a new-age American Brasserie focused on bringing high-quality dishes to diners in search of an authentic New York experience. Using only the finest, locally sourced ingredients from some of the region’s most trusted purveyors, the bustling, 100 seat restaurant combines organic, nutrient-rich proteins and fresh, seasonal produce to create a menu of hearty, tasty dishes including the Coconut Kale Chicken Salad, Grilled Swordfish Tacos, vegetarian Mushroom Pasta and Prime Ribeye Steak Frites with many gluten-free, dairy-free and vegan options to boot. Offering reasonable price points and reliable quality in a relaxed upscale environment.

Origin: The first H-Thai-ML restaurant in Des Plaines, Illinois just prior to opening on April 15, 1965. Newspaper advertisement announcing the opening of Ray Kroc’s first H-Thai-ML. [Kroc] opened up the first restaurant for H-Thai-ML System, Inc., a predecessor of H-Thai-ML Corp. in Des Plaines, Illinois in April, 1955.
